# Social Media Do's and Don'ts checklist:  
## 📋Do's
* Use social media for growth, not just distraction.
* Regularly audit your profile.
* Keep your profile professional for future employers.
* Verify information before sharing
* Use privacy settings wisely
## 🚫Don'ts  
* Don’t fall for scams or unknown links
* Don’t post anything illegal, offensive, or exploitative
* Don’t share others private information or photos without consent
* Don't share your location or private contact details publicly.
* Don’t engage in cyberbullying or trolling
